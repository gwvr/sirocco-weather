# Hide daily strip cards with insufficient hourly data

The last day(s) of the forecast often have sparse or missing hourly data from the UKMO model. Days with insufficient hourly coverage should either be hidden from the daily strip or shown in a visually distinct way (e.g. greyed out, no hourly panel).
