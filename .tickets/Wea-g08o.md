# Abbreviate day names and row labels on mobile

On narrow screens, full day names (e.g. 'Wednesday') overflow day cards, and row labels like 'Chance of precipitation' are too long. Abbreviate day names to 3 chars (Mon/Tue/etc, keep Today) and shorten row labels for mobile.
